Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 2026

Spa Booking Assistant is a Messenger automation tool used by spa and massage salons to communicate with customers and receive booking requests.

Information We Collect

The application may process Messenger user messages, selected language, booking request information, Facebook Page identifiers, and technical logs for debugging and service stability.

How Information Is Used

Information is used only for:

  • responding to customer inquiries
  • processing booking requests
  • sending notifications to salon administrators
  • improving multilingual support and service stability

Data Sharing

User data is not sold or shared with third parties for advertising purposes. Booking information may be forwarded to connected salon staff through integrated messaging services such as Telegram.

User Rights

Users may stop interacting with the application at any time by discontinuing use of the Messenger conversation.
